Awesome presentations, postcards, guided visits, games and more

mokap gives you the nuts and bolts, it's up to you to decide what to do with them. The limit is just your imagination!

For every person and purpose

mokap is great for making content for a myriad of purposes, and for everyone. No matter if you are preparing engaging content for your students, a presentation for coworkers, or designing a postcard to greet a friend on his new job. mokap is your app!

Fluent mobile user experience

With a beautiful and modern interface and a seamless workflow, mokap is designed to provide a great user experience. Just grab your coffee mug on one hand, your favourite Android tablet or phone on the other, and enjoy!

Features

Take photos and videos, or draw with your own finger to compose scenes

mokap provides you with multiple tools to get your scenes populated with images. And once you are done composing scenes, get them connected seamlessly.

Import rich assets from the community. To make life simpler.

Don't like spending countless hours searching for the right picture? Hate handling sound formats? Tired of not finding animated characters for your games? mokap provides you with thousands of graphic assets ready to use.

Edit your images with Pixlr Express (© Autodesk Inc.). Awesome effects at the reach of your hand

Pixlr Express is a great photo editing tool freely available in Android that works beautifully with mokap. Install it on your device to improve your mokap experience.

Ambient, animate and add interactivity

Enhance your scenes with animations, dynamic ambience effects and simple interactions. It is just a few touches ahead!

Once you are done, share your work! and astonish your friends and colleagues.
